One key. One program. Just tap to the beat to see your BPM!

Press space to a beat to view the BPM. That's literally all there is, aside from a cool animation that appears whenever you press space that I added.

To reset, move your cursor over the reset button and press space. Instead of clicking it, you press the same key.

*This project was made for Hack Club's onekey challenge.*
